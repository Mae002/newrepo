=========================
APP PLANNING & DESIGN
=========================

1. PROJECT IDEA
-------------------------
- What is your app called? AniVision 
- What type of app is it? (journal, listings, notes, etc.)
It lists animals and info about animals. 
- What will users use your app for?
They will use to store info they learn. 
2. DATA MODEL (WHAT ARE YOU STORING?)
-------------------------
- What is a single “entry” in your app?  An animal card which has 4 fields. 
- What fields will each entry have? (title, content, date, etc.) 
animal name, habitat, food, image
- How will entries be connected to a user?
if the user creates the entry they can edit and delete it
3. CORE FEATURES (CRUD)
-------------------------
- What can users CREATE? They can create a new animal card info
- What will they see on the main page (READ)? Animal cards that are preloaded and their own entries
- What can users EDIT (UPDATE)? They can edit only their own entry
- What can users DELETE? Only their own entry

4. USER FLOW
-------------------------
- What happens after a user logs in? They can view preloaded content, and choose to add animals through pressing a button. 
- What pages/routes will your app need? (dashboard, create, edit, etc.) 
dashboard, register, login, edit, delete, create
- Describe a typical user experience from login → using your app
When the log in they are redirected to dashboard and when they scroll down they can see and add animal info button and a logout if they choose to add animal they are taken to a different card where they are asked to fill out 4 fields and they can choose to add it or go back to animals. Each entry (created by the user) will have an edit and delete button. 
5. USER EXPERIENCE (UX)
-------------------------
- How should your app look and feel? Our app should feel easy and fun to use. 
- How will users navigate between pages? They can use buttons
- How will entries be displayed (list, cards, etc.)? Cards

6. OPTIONAL FEATURES
-------------------------
- What extra features might you add? (search, tags, sorting, etc.)
Perhaps an tags features to further categorize the animals.
